Red Side (Offensive)

The Red Side, also known as Offensive Security, focuses on simulating cyber attacks to test and improve an organization's defenses. Red Team members take on the role of adversaries, conducting ethical hacking, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments to identify weaknesses in systems, networks, and applications. Their goal is to provide insights into potential security gaps and help organizations strengthen their security posture by recommending improvements. By understanding the tactics, techniques, and procedures of real-world attackers, Red Team professionals help to enhance the overall resilience of the organization against cyber threats.

Penetration Tester

Penetration Testers, or ethical hackers, are experts in finding and exploiting vulnerabilities within an organization's systems, networks, and applications. They conduct simulated attacks to assess the security posture, identify weaknesses, and provide actionable recommendations for remediation. Penetration Testers use a variety of tools and techniques to mimic real-world cyber threats, helping organizations to understand potential risks and improve their defenses. Their work is crucial in proactively identifying security issues before they can be exploited by malicious actors.

Courses available:

  • Penetration Testing Basics

    Introduction to the fundamentals of penetration testing, covering methodologies, tools, and best practices.

  • Web Application Penetration Testing

    Focuses on identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities in web applications, including SQL injection, XSS, and CSRF.

Ethical Hacker

Ethical Hackers use their skills to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in an organization’s systems with permission, aiming to strengthen the security posture. They conduct thorough assessments of networks, applications, and devices, using the same techniques as malicious hackers. Ethical Hackers provide detailed reports of their findings and suggest measures to mitigate identified risks. Their proactive approach helps organizations stay ahead of potential threats and continuously improve their cybersecurity defenses.

Courses available:

  • Ethical Hacking Fundamentals

    Basics of ethical hacking, including legal considerations, reconnaissance, and basic exploitation techniques.

  • Advanced Ethical Hacking

    In-depth exploration of advanced hacking techniques, including social engineering, physical security, and post-exploitation.

Red Team Operator

Red Team Operators conduct advanced adversarial simulations to evaluate and improve an organization’s security defenses. They use a range of tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) to mimic real-world cyber threats, testing the effectiveness of defensive measures. Red Team Operators collaborate closely with Blue Teams to identify gaps and improve incident response capabilities. Their work is instrumental in providing a realistic assessment of an organization’s security posture and helping to develop robust defense strategies.

Courses available:

  • Red Teaming Fundamentals

    Introduction to the principles and practices of Red Teaming, including planning, execution, and reporting.

  • Social Engineering Tactics

    Strategies for exploiting human vulnerabilities through phishing, pretexting, and other social engineering methods.

Vulnerability Assessor

Vulnerability Assessors specialize in identifying and evaluating security weaknesses within an organization’s systems, networks, and applications. They conduct systematic scans and assessments to discover vulnerabilities and provide detailed reports on their findings. Vulnerability Assessors prioritize risks and recommend remediation measures to mitigate potential threats. Their work is essential in maintaining a proactive security posture by continuously monitoring for and addressing vulnerabilities before they can be exploited.

Courses available:

  • Vulnerability Assessment Fundamentals

    Basics of vulnerability assessment, including scanning tools, methodologies, and reporting techniques.

  • Advanced Vulnerability Assessment

    Advanced techniques for conducting thorough vulnerability assessments and interpreting complex findings.
